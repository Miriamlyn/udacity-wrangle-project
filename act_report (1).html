Insights¶

  1. The below pie chart shows that twitter for Iphone is the source with the highest tweet data. Note that this is not to say that this is completely without question as there could be other factors such as content that might contribute to twitter for Iphone being the major tweet source.

  1. The below scatter plot shows the correlation between retweets and favorite count(likes). There is a positive relation between likes and retweets as we can tell from the plot that tweets with more likes are more likely to get more retweets. This is to say as more retweets happen to posts made on @WeRateDogs, there will be more likes. This is also not to imply that there is causability.

  1. The bar chart below shows that the audience on @weratedogs twitter page seems to like doggo,puppo as the stage has 17000 retweets compared to other dog stages. This is ti say that the more images of doggo,puppo posted on the @WeRateDogs page, the more activity of retweets that the page will gather.

  1. In the above comparison of retweets and dog stages, we saw that there are more retweets on the doggo,puppo stage. In the below comparison between favorite_count(likes) and dog stages, there are over 40000 likes for doggo,puppo stage. So we can say that to get more likes and retweets on their page, @weratedogs might need to post more doggo,puppo images.
